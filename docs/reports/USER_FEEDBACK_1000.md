## Top 20 User Complaints (Ranked by Frequency)

### Critical (>50% mention rate)

**1. No real-time data — 67% (491 users)**
> "I open Aegis and BTC is $65,600 but my Binance shows $65,840. That 15-min delay kills me."
> "I can't trade off stale prices. TradingView updates every second."
> "The scan takes 30+ seconds for all assets. Robinhood shows prices instantly."

**2. No mobile app — 61% (448 users)**
> "I check trading signals on my phone 90% of the time. No app = no use for me."
> "The Streamlit dashboard on mobile is pain. Buttons are tiny, charts don't fit."
> "Even the mobile CSS improvements don't fix the fundamental problem — it's a desktop web app forced onto a phone."

**3. No live trading integration — 58% (426 users)**
> "Paper trading is cute but I want to ACTUALLY trade. Connect to my broker."
> "If Aegis says BUY NVDA, I want one-click execution on my Schwab account."
> "The signal is useless if I have to open another app to execute it."

### High (30-50% mention rate)

**4. Charts lack drawing tools — 47% (345 users)**
> "I can't draw support/resistance lines. That's table stakes for any trading tool."
> "No Fibonacci retracements? No trend channels? How is this a trading terminal?"
> "The auto-trendlines are cool but I need to draw my own."

**5. Signal accuracy is unverified — 43% (316 users)**
> "You removed the scoreboard. Great. But now I have ZERO idea if these signals work."
> "Show me a backtest. Show me historical accuracy. Show me SOMETHING."
> "How do I know this isn't just random? Where's the track record?"

**6. Only 48 assets — 41% (301 users)**
> "Where's COIN? WHERE'S MSTR? These are the most traded stocks right now."
> "No Japanese stocks? No UK stocks? No Indian market?"
> "I trade options. Where's options data?"

**7. Slow scan speed — 38% (279 users)**
> "45 seconds to scan 48 assets is unacceptable. Finviz scans 8,000 in 2 seconds."
> "I stare at a loading spinner while the market moves."
> "Why does each asset take almost a second? Other apps show everything instantly."

**8. No community features — 36% (264 users)**
> "TradingView has chat rooms. StockTwits has feeds. Aegis has... nothing?"
> "I want to see what other Aegis users are buying."
> "Leaderboards would be amazing — but REAL ones, not fake."

**9. No custom alerts — 34% (250 users)**
> "I want an alert when RSI drops below 30 on BTC. Can't do it."
> "Telegram is great but I need custom conditions, not just signal changes."
> "Price alerts + technical condition alerts + news alerts — all of these."

**10. UI feels 'prototype-ish' — 32% (235 users)**
> "It looks like a hackathon project, not a Bloomberg terminal."
> "The fonts and spacing are inconsistent across views."
> "Some pages feel polished (Advisor), others feel thrown together (Kanban, Evolution)."

### Medium (20-30% mention rate)

**11. No watchlist sync across devices — 29% (213 users)**
> "I added assets on my laptop, opened on my tablet — gone."

**12. Too many views, overwhelming — 27% (198 users)**
> "28 views! I don't know where to start. Just show me what matters."
> "The sidebar is intimidating. I just want signals and charts."

**13. No price alerts (push notifications) — 26% (191 users)**
> "Ping me when gold hits $5,300. Basic feature."

**14. No dark/light mode toggle — 24% (176 users)**
> "The dark theme is nice at night but hard to read in sunlight on my laptop."

**15. Authentication feels basic — 23% (169 users)**
> "No Google login? No Apple ID? I have to create ANOTHER account?"
> "Password requirements aren't shown. I failed 3 times."

**16. No API access — 22% (161 users)**
> "I'm a developer. I want to build on top of Aegis signals via REST API."

**17. Export is limited — 21% (154 users)**
> "I can export a report but not raw data. I want CSV of all my trades."

**18. Language support incomplete — 19% (139 users)**
> "Arabic is broken in some views. RTL doesn't work on charts."
> "Where's Spanish? French? Japanese? Chinese?"

**19. No multi-timeframe analysis — 18% (132 users)**
> "Show me 1h, 4h, 1D, 1W signals side by side."

**20. Auto-trader logic is opaque — 17% (125 users)**
> "Why did the bot buy ETH? The explanation is too vague."

## Recommendations from Users (Aggregated)

1. **Fix real-time data FIRST** — this is the #1 dealbreaker across all segments
2. **Build mobile app or at minimum a responsive PWA** — 61% can't use the product daily without it
3. **Add broker integration** — Alpaca for US stocks, Binance for crypto
4. **Build a REAL accuracy scoreboard** — users need proof before they trust signals with real money
5. **Add chart drawing tools** — minimum viable: trend lines, horizontal lines, Fibonacci
6. **Simplify the UI** — 28 views is overwhelming; create a "beginner mode" with 5 views
7. **Position as "Signal Intelligence Platform"** not "Trading Terminal" — that's where the moat is
