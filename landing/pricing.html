Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free tier actually free forever? ▼
Yes. The Recruit tier is permanently free with no credit card required. You get full AI signals, paper trading, social sentiment, and all core features. We believe in earning your upgrade, not forcing it.
What's the difference between free and Pro? ▼
Pro (Operator) adds portfolio optimization with 4 strategies (Max Sharpe, Min Variance, etc.), the Strategy Lab for backtesting custom strategies, efficient frontier charts, email morning briefs, Telegram notifications, and export reports. The core AI signals and paper trading are free.
Can I try Pro before paying? ▼
Yes! Every new account gets a 14-day Pro trial. No credit card required. If you don't upgrade, you seamlessly downgrade to the free tier with no data loss.
Is this a broker? Can I trade real money? ▼
Aegis Terminal is an advisory and educational platform, not a broker. Currently, it supports paper trading (simulated). Broker integration (starting with Alpaca for US stocks and crypto) is on the roadmap for Q2 2026.
What assets does Aegis cover? ▼
Currently 12+ assets: Gold, BTC, ETH, Silver, Oil, Natural Gas, S&P 500, NASDAQ, Copper, Platinum, Wheat, and EUR/USD. Users can add/remove assets via the Watchlist Manager. US stock coverage is on the roadmap.
How accurate are the AI signals? ▼
Check the Prediction Report Card inside the app. Every signal is recorded and validated against real market outcomes within 48 hours. The accuracy data is transparent and updated continuously. Past performance does not guarantee future results.
